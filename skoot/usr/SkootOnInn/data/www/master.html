You can control your virtual persona, explore and investigate the world and communicate with people. This is done by typing lines of text at the bottom of the browser window.

The lines typed are imperative statements (commands) like 'look', 'go north', 'smile at the dog'. If the software understands what you are trying to do, it will cause your persona to do it: that is how you interact with the virtual environment.

To look around,

> look
> look at lisa
To move around,
> north
> go iron door
To socialize,
> grin broadly
> nod to michael "I think this'll work."
> ask gifford "So, what is this anyway?"
> smile at evelyn "This is a good soup."
Parts of speech

Feel free to try things. You can use phrases that describe how you say something such as...

> remark, comment, inquire, ask, state, imply, etc.
You can also communicate with facial expression...
> frown, sneer, smirk, smile, blink, etc.
